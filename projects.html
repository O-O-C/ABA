MyNigeria

is an initiative of the ABA organization working to expose children (age 6-12) to their cultural roots. Through summer school and cultural exchange tours amongst others, MyNigeria inspires teenagers to be culture inquisitive by creating a learning platform that would deliver the best possible learning opportunity on all aspects of Nigerian culture, however diverse.

Vision

to achieve a high level of cultural awareness amongst every teenager in Nigeria.

Mission

to deliver a learning opportunity for teenagers to experience all aspects of every Nigerian culture.

Project Description

A summer school initiative that would span a period of two (2) weeks in conducive learning facilities. The children would be exposed to various cultures: their languages, history and art etc. Maximizing the use of audiovisuals and expert facilitators, the kids are guaranteed an interactive and memorable learning experience. Inclusive in this programme is a cultural exchange and display among participants which is going to be competitive, with the wining team presented with gifts.
This programme is scheduled for August/September 2018 with the final event holding at Terra Kulture, Victoria Island, Lagos

Curriculum

Several children do not have the opportunity of being properly exposed to their own culture and its identity elements (language, art, history, dressing etc.) And there is a high need for children to understand the details of our individual culture and also of other tribes’ existent in Nigeria, as this is one of the ways to foster cultural integration amongst the richness of Nigeria’s diversity.
